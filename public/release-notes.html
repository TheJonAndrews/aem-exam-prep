  • ↑
    Exercise a2 — Roles, Groups & Permissions rewritten with 11 steps: explicit credentials for the test user, explanation of why built-in impersonation may fail on local SDK (use incognito instead), and a breakdown of the three key JCR privileges (jcr:read, jcr:modifyProperties, rep:write)

    Exercise e5 — Feature Recommendation Matrix expanded from a simple quiz to a 12-step learning exercise: each scenario now explains WHY that feature is the correct choice, with a bonus A/B testing scenario covering Launches + ContextHub/Target
  • ↑
